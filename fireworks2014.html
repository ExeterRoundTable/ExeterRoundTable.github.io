Location & Transport

Exeter Round Table’s Annual charity fireworks display is being held at Westpoint again this year. This spectacular event has grown to become the biggest fireworks display in Exeter, and taken on a new dimension since moving to Westpoint three years ago.

Stage Coach Buses

The route will be St David's Station, Exeter Bus Station bay 7, Sowton Park & Ride, Westpoint. 

X52 buses will be running every 15-20 minutes from St David's Station 1700-1855, from Exeter Bus Station 1710-1905 and from Sowton Park & Ride 1720-1915, returning from Westpoint at regular intervals from 2000 until 2120. 

The cheap £1.50 return ticket will be available on the normal 52 service as well as the X52 

Opening times

Please plan to arrive on site by 6.45pm at the latest, to allow time to park and pass through the ticket gates before the fireworks go off at 7.30pm.

Please note that we will need to close the main gates to road traffic at 7.15pm due to the location of the fireworks firing area. If you arrive after 7.15pm we cannot guarantee that you will be able to get onto site even if you have bought advance tickets. Sparklers are not permitted at this event.

 

Disabled Access
Disabled 'Blue badge' drivers please follow signs for disabled parking and easy site access. Most areas are accessible by wheelchair via the tarmac roads. Disabled toilets available within main Westpoint arena building.

Parking
Free car parks on site. Disabled 'Blue badge' drivers please follow signs for disabled parking
